How it works

  • Click Start to begin.
  • If you leave the set on All sentences, the round mixes every sentence in the app.
  • You have 1 minute 30 seconds per round.
  • Sentences are shuffled and recycled as needed during the round.
  • Each sentence can be answered twice.
  • You get 10 points on the first attempt, 5 points on the second.
  • Press Enter to submit, or use the button.
  • Replay the audio whenever you want.
Punctuation is ignored. Apostrophes are accepted in curly or straight form.
